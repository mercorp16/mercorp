FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is the BBQ flavor spicy?

Our BBQ flavor is crafted to be savory and smoky rather than spicy, making it enjoyable for a wide audience. It has a rich, sweet, and tangy profile with just a hint of warmth from paprika.

What makes your BBQ seasoning 'natural'?

Our seasoning is made from real food ingredients like smoked paprika, tomato powder, and onion powder. We do not use artificial flavors, flavor enhancers like MSG, or synthetic colors.

How does this BBQ flavor compare to those on potato chips?

You get the same robust, satisfying BBQ flavor you love, but on a superior base. Our crackers are baked from organic whole-grain brown rice, making them a lighter, crispier, and more wholesome alternative to deep-fried potato chips.
